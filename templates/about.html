About Me

Hi! My name is Michael Scheie and I am a Senior Software Engineer! I grew up in Saint Louis Park, MN and always had an interest in technology. That was true even through college because in 2019 I graduated with a B.A. in Computer Science at Gustavus Adolphus. Since then my passion for programming (and technology) still continues to grow as I explore new opportunities through work while also exploring on my own through side-projects (like creating this website)!

For almost two years I fostered Kittens through Twin Cities Pet Rescue. I stopped because we had a puppy on the way! Kovu is (now) a 10 month old miniature Australian Shepard! He's been quite the handful as he's still a puppy and has lots and LOTS of energy!
